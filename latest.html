April 27 2025

Porsche 911 GT3 R driver Ayhancan Güven celebrates commanding first win

Turkish driver Ayhancan Güven secured his first-ever DTM victory at the Motorsport Arena Oschersleben, becoming the first Turkish race winner in DTM history. Driving the No. 90 Porsche 911 GT3 R for Manthey EMA, Güven executed a flawless overcut strategy to surpass polesitter Jules Gounon, finishing 2.695 seconds ahead. His teammate, Thomas Preining, completed the podium in third place, marking a successful outing for the Manthey EMA team.

Taycan Turbo GT Sets Blazing Lap Record at Interlagos

Porsche has once again pushed the limits of electric performance. Behind the wheel of the new Taycan Turbo GT with the Weissach package, professional racer Felipe Nasr clocked a blistering 1:42.1 lap time at Brazil’s famous Interlagos Circuit in São Paulo. That makes it the fastest lap ever recorded by an electric vehicle on that track.
