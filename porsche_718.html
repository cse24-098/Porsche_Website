Meet the Porsche 718

The Porsche 718 Cayman is the kind of car that makes you want to take the long way home. Light, fast, and razor-sharp around corners, it's a car built for pure driving pleasure. And it looks just as good parked outside a caf√© as it does tearing up a mountain road. The Porsche 718 is a lineage of mid-engine sports cars that emphasizes lightweight design, precision handling, and pure driving pleasure. The current 718 family consists of the Cayman (coupe) and Boxster (convertible).

The 718 name harks back to Porsche's legendary race cars of the 1950s, particularly the 718 RSK that dominated in motorsport. In 2016, Porsche revived the "718" nameplate to represent the modern Cayman and Boxster models, now powered by turbocharged four-cylinder engines.The 718 lineup features a mid-engine layout, ensuring near-perfect weight distribution and delivering sharper, more agile cornering. Under the hood, it offers impressive turbocharged engines with 2.0L and 2.5L flat-four options, depending on the model. When it comes to performance, the 718 is no slouch: 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) comes in around 4.9 seconds, with a thrilling top speed exceeding 275 km/h (170 mph). This combination of heritage, precision, and raw speed makes the 718 a true driver's car.

But the magic of the 718 doesn't stop with just one model!

Porsche offers a variety of 718 variants, each with its own unique personality, performance upgrades, and style. Whether you're chasing raw speed, open-top freedom, or track-ready precision, there's a 718 built just for you.
